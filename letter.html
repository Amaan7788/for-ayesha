Dear Ayesha,

I hope this brings a smile to your face.


When our families met at my home last week, I was so incredibly happy to finally see you. I'll admit, I wanted to talk to you so much, but the excitement and a little bit of shyness got the better of me. Even though we didn't get to share words that evening, just seeing you made it a night I will never forget.


Since my family is visiting your home today and I couldn't be there, I wanted to send a piece of my heart along with these flowers. I wanted to create a moment just for the two of us, free from any shyness.


Ayesha, we are standing at the very beginning of a beautiful new chapter. Even in that quiet silence last week, I felt a spark that I haven't stopped thinking about. You have completely captured my mind and my heart.


So, away from the crowd, just between you and me...


Will you walk this beautiful journey of life with me? Will you let me be the one who makes you smile, who stands by you through everything, and who loves you more with each passing day?


I am so genuinely proud to be engaged to you, and I cannot wait for the day the shyness fades and we get to share our thoughts, our laughs, and our dreams. Thank you for coming into my life.

With all my heart and affection,
Amaan
