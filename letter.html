~ A Letter For You ~
Dear Ayesha,

I hope this brings a smile to your face.


When our families met at my home last month, I was so incredibly happy to finally see you. I'll admit, I wanted to talk to you so much, but the excitement and a little bit of shyness got the better of me. Even though we didn't get to share words that evening, just seeing you made it a night I will never forget.


Since my family is visiting your home today and I couldn't be there, I wanted to send a piece of my heart along with these flowers. I wanted to create a moment just for the two of us, free from any shyness.


Ayesha, we are standing at the very beginning of a beautiful new chapter. Even in that quiet silence last month, I felt a spark that I haven't stopped thinking about. You have completely captured my mind and my heart.


So, away from the crowd, just between you and me...


We began this journey with the blessings of our families, but today, I want to ask for your heart. Will you walk hand-in-hand with me through life? Will you let me love you, protect you, and make you the happiest person every single day?


I am so genuinely proud to be engaged to you, and I cannot wait for the day the shyness fades and we get to share our thoughts, our laughs, and our dreams. Thank you for coming into my life.

With all my heart and affection,
Amaan
made with care, for you
